About

Research-driven. Impact-oriented.

I'm a Product Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria with a background spanning UX research, interaction design, and design systems. I work at the intersection of user empathy and business strategy — translating complex needs into digital experiences that convert and delight.

From redesigning core features for platforms with 1M+ users to crafting AI-powered connection flows and healthcare data tools, I bring end-to-end ownership across discovery, prototyping, and delivery.

My work is grounded in accessibility-first principles, cross-functional collaboration with PMs and engineers, and a commitment to measurable outcomes — not just polished screens.
